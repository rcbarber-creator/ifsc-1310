    • Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

    Progressive Enhancement is a must have for the designing of a website. It is the process of designing a website with the intention of making the website accessible and readable for as many people as possible. This is done by centering every factor of the site around the content, ensuring that the user has a positive and engaging experience with the site. It's an excellent tool for making sure that the site works to the best of its ability while making sure that the cite's appeal spreads to a multiple amount of users
